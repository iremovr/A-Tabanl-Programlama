What is the Pomodoro Method?

The Pomodoro Method is a technique developed to manage time more efficiently. This method recommends dividing tasks into a cycle of 25 minutes of work followed by a short break.

How does it work?

  1. Choose a task to do.
  2. Set a timer for 25 minutes.
  3. Focus on the task until the timer expires.
  4. When the time is up, take a 5-minute break.
  5. After 4 Pomodoro cycles, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).
